COMP1100 Programming as Problem Solving (Feb 2023- Jul 2023)
======

Programming as Problem Solving. An introductory course for new Computer Science students focusing on problem-solving and functional programming, utilising Haskell as the programming language. 

I was responsible for a wide range of tasks but not limited to:
- Effectively explain complex concepts in a concise manner tailored toward each student.
- Actively providing assistance, encouragement, and support to students during and outside of lab sessions.
- Evaluating and providing constructive feedback on assignment items for students.
